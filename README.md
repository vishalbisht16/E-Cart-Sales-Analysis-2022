# E-Cart-Sales-Analysis-2022
# Project Title: E-Cart Sales Analysis 2022
# Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze the annual sales data for 2022 to gain actionable insights into customer behavior and identify growth opportunities for 2023.

# Tools Used
Microsoft Excel: Utilized for data cleaning, transformation, and analysis.

Pivot Tables & Charts: Employed to create interactive visualizations and summarize large datasets effectively.

# Key Analytical Insights
Sales Performance Trends: Conducted a comprehensive comparison of monthly sales and order volumes to identify peak performance periods.

Customer Demographics: Analyzed purchasing patterns by gender to determine the primary customer base.

Order Fulfillment: Evaluated order status distribution throughout 2022 to assess operational efficiency.

Geographic Contribution: Identified the top 10 states driving the highest total revenue.

Customer Segmentation: Explored the correlation between age groups, gender, and purchasing frequency.

Channel Efficiency: Determined the sales channel that contributes the most to overall revenue.

Product Performance: Analyzed category-wise sales data to pinpoint top-selling product segments.
